FAQs

What is Fudap?

Fudap is a business to business food delivery service. We partner with restaurants and food vendors to help them get their orders delivered to their customers. Restaurants and vendors can request for a rider, track orders and easily get paid.

How can I use Fudap?

If you're a food restaurant/vendor, download our Vendor App. Sign up easily and start requesting for riders to get orders delivered to your customers.

How do I pay for an orders

Once you request for a rider, you will get a calculated delivery fee based on the customers location.

Does Fudap do Pay on Delivery

Yes! Fudap has a pay on delivery feature. We can help you collect your payment from your customer. You can view and manage your payments within your Vendor App.

How do I resolve complaint?

There is a dedicated team of customer hapiness that available to help you answer your questions or complaints. You can reach us via your Vendor App and other official contact channels.
